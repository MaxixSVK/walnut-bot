:large_orange_diamond: This server follows a 4 strike system applied to major offenses.

1st Strike - One hour mute
2nd Strike - 24 hour mute
3rd Strike - 48 hour mute
4th Strike - Ban

:large_orange_diamond: Please note that this is just a standard system, our moderators are free to choose the punishment as they feel necessary, subject to the incident.

:large_orange_diamond: A strike can be retracted after a user has shown genuine improvement after a period of time.

:large_orange_diamond: Rapid fire violations can lead to an immediate ban, regardless of prior strikes
